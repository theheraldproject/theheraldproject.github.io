The Composition relationship indicates that an object consists of a number of other objects.

The interpretation of a Composition relationship is that the whole or part of the source element is composed of the whole of the target element.

A specific kind of server can be modeled as a node composed of a device and system software implying an existence dependency between individual servers of that kind and the individual devices and system software instances of which they consist.

A Composition relationship is always allowed between two instances of the same element type.

Category:

Structural.

Examples:

An Application Component may be composed of two or more sub-components.
