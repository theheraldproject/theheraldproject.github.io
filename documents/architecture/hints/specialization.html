The Specialization relationship indicates that an object is a specialization of another object.

A Specialization relationship is always allowed between two instances of the same element type.

Alternatively, a Specialization relationship can be expressed by nesting the specialized element inside the generic element.

Category:

Other.

Examples:

A "Take out travel insurance" and a "Take out luggage insurance" process are a specialization of a more generic "Take out insurance" process.
